What is TEDx?

In the spirit of discovering and spreading ideas

TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event — TEDxSelwynHouseSchool Youth — features live speakers sharing ideas that spark deep discussion and connection.

The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events, including ours, are self-organized.
